INTRODUCTION

Digital Marketing encompasses all marketing effforts that promote our business, product or brand using electronic devices or digital channels like internet, websites, email and many more. It is a online marketing like search marketing, email marketing, social media marketing. The term marketing is not just about promoting and advertising. It is a complex concepts where we send across the right message to the right person via the right medium. There are various types of devices so that our audience experiences our brand as they interact with the website through digital devices including mobile phones, desktop, tablets, etc. Most interaction on digital devices through browser or application from the major platforms like Google, Facebook, Instagram, You tube, Twitter, Linked in.

What is SEO

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization.It is the process of optimizing content, technical set up, and reach of our website so that our pages appear on the top of a search engine result for specific keywords. The ultimate goal is to attract visitors to our website when they search for product or service related to our business. SEO-short for search engine optimization-is about helping search engines understand your content, and helping users find your site and make a decision about whether they should visit your site through a search engine.The better visibility your pages have in search results, the more likely you are to be found and visited.

Types of SEO

There are three types of SEO

  • On-pages SEO
  • Off-pages SEO
  • Technical SEO

On-pages SEO

On-page SEO (also called on-site SEO) is the process of optimizing webpages and their content for both search engines and users. It can help rank pages higher on Google and drive more organic traffic. Common tasks associated with on-page SEO include optimizing for search intent, title tags, internal links, and URLs.

Technical SEO

It focuses on the backend of websites image compression, CSS file Optimization and structured data are different forms of SEO that can increase our website loading speed that is an important ranking factor for search engine.Technical SEO is the process of optimizing a website's technical aspects to improve its search engine ranking. The goal is to make it easier for search engines to crawl and index a website's pages. This can lead to increased website traffic.

Technical SEO includes:-

  1. Page Speed:- Making a website faster so its easier to navigate.
  2. Site Security:- Using SSL, security to keep a website secure for visitors.
  3. Mobile friendliness:- Ensuring a website is responsive on mobile devices.
  4. Site Architecture:- Examining a website's architecture to eliminate friction points.

PPC(pay-per click)

PPC stands for pay-per click which is a digital marketing model where
advertise pay a fee each time when someone clicks on their ad. It is way to
rank a website in search engine.The pay-per-click model is offered primarily
by search engines (e.g., Google) and social networks (e.g., Facebook). Google
Ads, Facebook Ads, and Twitter Ads are the most popular platforms for PPC
advertising.

How PPC works

Advertise on keywords and only pay whenever someone visit any website or
any platform and click on ad an then they pay whenever they want.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a free service that measures and reports on website traffics and user visibility.
It is most important for website where we can see who visit in our website. It works by adding a tracking code to each page of a website and collecting information about how user internet with the site.

How does Google Analytics work ?

The main goal of google analytics purpose is to track our website using and we need add a snippet of javascript code to our website's pages. When a user visits a page with this code snippet the uses a javascript file to start tracking data and send it to google analytics.
Once google analytics has collected data from out website, the data will populate the report with Google Analytics.
